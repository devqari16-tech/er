Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How long does a full cleaning service take?

A complete cleaning service typically takes 3-6 hours depending on the property size and condition. For larger commercial properties, it may take longer. We never rush our work to ensure the highest quality results.

What cleaning products do you use?

We exclusively use premium, professional-grade cleaning products from trusted brands. All our products are eco-friendly, non-toxic, and safe for all surfaces. We can also accommodate specific product requests or sensitivities for clients with allergies.

Do you offer recurring cleaning services?

Yes! We offer flexible recurring cleaning schedules including daily, weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly options. Our recurring clients enjoy priority scheduling and discounted rates.

How often should I have my property professionally cleaned?

For residential properties, we recommend a deep clean every 2-4 weeks. Commercial properties typically require more frequent cleaning, such as daily or weekly services. The frequency depends on property size, usage, and specific needs.

Are your cleaning teams insured and background-checked?

Absolutely! All our cleaning professionals are fully insured, bonded, and undergo comprehensive background checks. We prioritize your safety and security, ensuring only trusted team members enter your property.

Do you clean government and institutional facilities?

Yes! We have extensive experience cleaning government buildings, schools, hospitals, and other institutional facilities. We understand the unique requirements and compliance standards for public sector properties.
